Description

This is how you make the wonderful dish Potato and leek soup

Ingredients

  • 8 Potatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 leek
  • 1 Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chopped chili fruit
  • 1 Litre of veggie broth
  • 2 dl cream
  • Olive oil
  • Thyme (Optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and chop the onion and leek in pieces. Stirfry all of it together with the chili and some oil.
  2. Pour the broth into the cooking pot.
  3. Add some thyme if you want.
